How to play Square Up!

1. Users enter a username and select a color to play in.
2. The chosen color will customize the game’s visuals.
4. Players type the words upon seeing them.
5. If spelled correctly and typed before their
   opponent, the box in which the word appeared will turn to
   the player’s color.
6. Players have 1 minute to type as many words and fill as
   many boxes with their color
   as possible.
7. When time is up, the player with more boxes in their color
   wins!
