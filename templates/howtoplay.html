How to play Square Up!

Users enter a username and select a color to play in.
The chosen color will determine the game’s visuals, as it customizes
the gameplay to match.
Randomly generated words will appear in a playing grid.
Players type the words upon seeing them.
If spelled correctly and typed before their opponent, the box
in which the word appeared will turn to the player’s color.
Players have 1 minute to type as many words and fill as many boxes
with their color as possible.
When time is up, the player with more boxes in their color wins!
